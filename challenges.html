Apps for Detroit challenges

Representatives from City Hall, Michigan Community Resources , Data Driven Detroit, and others have shared problems to solve with digital apps. You absolutely can submit apps that address other challenges. The apps you build will be judged by their creativity, impact and civic focus.

Have questions about these challenges or anything else? Feel free to contact us at detroit@codeforamerica.org.

Help citizens track progress and contact City Council members and other elected officials on ideas and changes to the new City charter
Help citizens report potholes or other city service needs
We don't know much about internet access in Detroit. Find a way to map or document who has access to a wireless network and a computer.
Aggregate calls and notices (local and national) for conferences, proposals and advertise these opportunities in Detroit to relevant organizations.
Help Detroiters visualize complex issues. Tell accessible stories that push the city forward. Tell the story of redlining, a shrinking city and lost tax base. Visualize the effects and costs of a population that is not insured for health, auto or home.
National databases store nursing home and hospital information which could be of great use to Detroiters if aggregated at the local level.
Connect Detroiters to community organizations! Right now its difficult to know where they are or how to connect.
There are many community events in Detroit — help Detroiters find out when and where its happening!
Support local entrepreneurs by helping make available goods and services from local businesses more transparent.
Help Detroiters navigate who/where to call or visit when you need to report an issue (beyond 311).
There's great fresh produce in Detroit. Help citizens find out information (location/hours/directions) about local farmer's markets, community gardens and produce stands are in their communities.
Help Detroiters figure out where parks are, what activities are there and when special events will happen!
Find out where the many local art galleries/studios are to view or create art!
Help Detroit's youth locate and find out about ecreational and after-school activities.
Create an easy way to upload and tag mobile photos on a map to mark things like "the best of Detroit" or "abandoned property" or "best street art."
Show what streets flood when it rains!
Help Detroit's cyclists map their regular bike-to-work routes and times.
Create commercial property and startup/entrepreneur space matching service — connecting those who need commercial space with available property!
Voting information on a mobile app!
